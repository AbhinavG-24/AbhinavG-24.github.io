About Me

Who I Am

A builder driven by impact, not just code.

I'm a 3rd-year B.Tech Computer Science student at Jaypee Institute of Information Technology (JIIT), Noida, maintaining a CGPA of 9.3/10. I combine strong fundamentals in algorithms and ML with hands-on project experience across full-stack development and data science.

My flagship project AirOptima — an AI-driven pollution control system for Delhi-NCR — won the Climate Data Hackathon Delhi 2026 organized by EPAM, Climate Collective, and JIIT. It uses a Random Forest Classifier to intelligently dispatch water sprinklers across 10 Delhi zones based on live AQI, PM2.5, PM10, weather, and traffic data.

Beyond building, I co-founded A2Study Material, a grassroots academic platform that grew to support 600+ CSE/IT students at JIIT with curated notes and exam resources — proving engineering can drive community impact too.

I'm an active competitive programmer on LeetCode (230+ problems), Codeforces, and GeeksforGeeks, constantly sharpening my algorithmic thinking.
